Welcome to MatchFour!

How to Play:

  1. Click to select a color
  2. Click on a box within the first row (your currently selected color will show before you make your guess)
  3. Fill in all four boxes with your guesses, then click the green check mark to confirm your guess
  4. 4 circles will display next to your confirmed guess. Black = Correct slot and color, White = Correct Color, Wrong Slot
  5. Correctly guess all 4 colors to win!
